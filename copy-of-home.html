Idea 1 - Post-Discharge Readmissions
Project's Central Problem and Goal: 
Problem Statement: 
How can we, through innovative approaches, decrease preventable hospital readmissions that stem from inadequate post-discharge support and information? 
“How might we reduce Post-Discharge Readmissions?”
Core Objective: To identify the fundamental causes of readmissions and to develop patient-centric interventions.
Research Questions:
1. Fundamental knowledge of medical processes, common chronic disease management models, and existing telehealth technologies.
2. In-depth understanding of the specific discharge needs for different diseases (e.g., heart disease, diabetes).
3. Study of patients' psychological states and behavioral habits after hospital discharge.
4. Exploration of the role of family caregivers in preventing readmissions.
5. Understanding the latest applications of artificial intelligence in health management.
6. Analysis of the current information gaps between hospitals, communities, and homes.
